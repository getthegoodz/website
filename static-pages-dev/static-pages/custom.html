CUSTOM GOODZ FAQ

What is your minimum order quantity for custom Goodz?

Custom orders start at just 50 units!

Do you make custom mini milk crates and crate sets?

Yes! We can make mini milk crates with custom logos in a variety of colors, and pack them with up to 10 custom Goodz. Get in touch with us at info@getthegoodz.com or fill out the below for more information.  

How long does it take to get them made?

Larger orders (>200 Goodz) usually take about 4 weeks to have in hand. Smaller orders (<200 Goodz) can take between 5-6 weeks, unless we are able to combine them with another order.

What will you need from me to place an order?

We just need your designs for the front and the back (or you can use our standard back art). We have a template we can send you, but generally speaking the canvas is a 65mm square with rounded edges. Your designs should be submitted as 300 DPI .PNG files measuring at least 300 x 300 pixels.

Can you create a custom landing page for my Goodz?

Absolutely! We can design a custom landing page for your Goodz that’s ideal for sharing content such as playlists, photos, event details, and other exclusive media. Your landing page will be tailored to showcase the content you want to share, creating a seamless and interactive experience for your audience.

Can I link my Goodz to existing online content?

Yes, you can! Simply provide the URL for the web content you want to link, whether it’s a website, playlist, video, or any other online resource. We’ll set up your Goodz to direct users there with a quick tap.

Can I update the content on my Goodz over time??

Yes! The content your Goodz points to can be updated at any time, allowing you to continually share new music, videos, or event information with your audience.
