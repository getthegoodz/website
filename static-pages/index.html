GOODZ FAQ

What the heck are these things?

Goodz are wooden collectibles that link to your favorite digital audio content. They let you collect and share your music in physical form without the hassle of traditional media like cassettes or vinyl records.

How do I access the content on my Goodz?

Simply tap your Good against your phone's NFC reader for a few seconds, and your content will be delivered! For more instructions on how to properly tap your Goodz, please click here to visit our support section.

Couldn't I just text a link to a friend and bypass this whole thing?

Yes! And probably you already do that. By all means, keep doing so. Goodz are for people who want to collect music in physical form either for themselves or to share with their friends and loved ones. If that doesn’t sound like fun, then Goodz might not be for you!

Are Goodz sustainable?

Sustainability is integral to Goodz, influencing every aspect of our practices.

Our collectibles are crafted from pieces of basswood, measuring 2.5" x 2.5" x ~1/8". Basswood, chosen for its rapid growth, stands out as an exceptionally sustainable wood crop.In our commitment to environmental responsibility, we employ a UV printing process. This method surpasses traditional inks in eco-friendliness by being entirely VOC-free. Unlike inks with volatile organic compounds (VOCs), which include alcohols, glycols, esters, hydrocarbons, and ethers, our UV printing uses UV light for drying the ink. This innovation minimizes environmental impact by avoiding the release of substances that, when evaporated during printing, can harm both our atmosphere and our health.

Our crates are made out of PLA (Polyactic Acid) plastic, a biodegrable, bioactive, and carbon neutral plastic originally derived from renewable resources like corn starch or sugar cane. Taking our commitment a step further, we exclusively use only recycled PLA in our manufacturing.

Finally, we prioritize using both recycled and biodegradable materials in our packaging whenever possible.

At Goodz, we strive to not only create exceptional collectibles but also to do so with a conscious and sustainable approach, safeguarding the well-being of our planet and its inhabitants.
